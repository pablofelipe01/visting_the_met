-Project Description:
    This pre & visiting app helps you make the most in your visit to the MET. 
    You will find everything you need to know in a very logical manner. 
    An even more, you will be able to make a print of your favorite piece of 
    art on a High-quality canvas.

-User Stories

    --MVP Goals
        As a Visitor, I Want my app to tell me the Art collections at the MET with a scroll button.
        As a Visitor, I Want my app to let me choose the collection department (these are locations at the Met).
        As a Visitor, I want my app to show me the Date/era of the collection.
        As a Visitor, I want my app to tell me the geographic location of a specific collection.
        As a Visitor, I want my app to show me all the information about my pick.
        As a Visitor, I Want my app to tell me MET opening and closing hours.
        As a Visitor, I Want my app to send me to a buy ticket site.

    --Stretch Goals
        As a Visitor, I Want to send prints to a High profile printing company!
